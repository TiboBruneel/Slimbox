About

Slimbox

SlimBox is a school project made by Tibo Sevenhant for the module 'Project 1' at Howest MCT.

SlimBox is a smart device speaker that can play media wireless through Bluetooth, can measure the current temperature, can change the volume through a button, has RGB led's where the color depends on the status,... It is also connected to an online dashboard with all different kinds of information and settings.

Slimbox is of course a prototype, but if you want to create one yourself, make sure to check out the instructables link.
Good luck!
